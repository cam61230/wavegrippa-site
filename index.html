The Story

Producer. DJ. wavegrippa.

wavegrippa is an electronic music producer and multi-genre DJ from the UK. Started making tunes in a bedroom (as is tradition), graduated to making tunes in a slightly bigger room with better monitors.

The sound? Hard to pin down. Could be deep rolling house one minute, face-melting DnB the next, with a cheeky garage bootleg thrown in for good measure. If you want to be put in a genre box, you've come to the wrong place, mate.

Currently riding high on the wavegrippa theme tune — universally agreed (by one person) to be the best production yet. Anti-corporate and absolutely not taking ourselves too seriously.
